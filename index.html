About Me

I have had a love and interest for technology since I was a young child. Ever since I could remember, I was fascinated with computers and their potential. As I got older, I realized I could make this my career by combining my interest with technology and helping others. My goal in life is to use my skills for good by helping others build their dreams.


I graduated from Clemson University with a B.S. in Computer Information Systems and a minor in Cybersecurity in 2021. Since then, I have worked in project management, web design, consulting, and development in the telecommunications field and drivers services software.


In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, working on embroidery projects, and teaching young students color guard. I also enjoy hanging out with my cat Marty! During my schooling years, I also played the violin and timpani. During the duration of my college years, I took private lessons and played with the CU Symphonic Orchestra.


I am looking to transition my career to something that is more focused on informing users how to use technical products with my expertise. I want to combine my technical and functional skills from schooling, project management, and development into something that can benefit end-users.
